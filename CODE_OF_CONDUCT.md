# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Hyperstrate to be a useful, respectful, and welcoming project for everyone.

Contributors and maintainers agree to participate in a way that is considerate, collaborative, and free from harassment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Focus discussions on what is best for the project and its users.
- Assume good intent, and clarify before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private user data in public project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or limit participation when behavior harms the project community.

Report concerns to `security@hyperstrate.ai`.
